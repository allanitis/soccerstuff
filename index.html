About this Site

On each of the pages about positions, (Forwards, Midfielders, Defenders, and Goalkeepers), you can expect to see a bit about what the position does, one of the best players of that position, and what makes them so good. You will also find a YouTube video on some highlights related to that position.

The other pages are more about preparing and improving your game. These pages contain information about various drills and gear that can give your game a major boost.

While Ronaldo and Messi are definitely the best two players in the world, it is heavily debated who is better than the other. Many Messi supporters believe that because of his world-class dribbling ability and passing vision Messi is better, while Ronaldo supporters will point out that his powerful shots with his left and right foot in addition to his ability to score from almost any position in the attacking third, Ronaldo surely takes the prize.

You can't be told who is better. You have to decide for yourself! The video nearby contains some highlights from each player. If you want to view it on YouTube, just give this link a click and enjoy the amazing skill that is like a second language to these players.
