About this Site

On each of the pages about positions, (Forwards, Midfielders, Defenders, and Goalkeepers), you can expect to see a bit about what the position does, one of the best players of that position, and what makes them so good. You will also find a YouTube video on some highlights related to that position.

The other pages are more about preparing and improving your game. These pages contain information about various drills and gear that can give your game a major boost.
