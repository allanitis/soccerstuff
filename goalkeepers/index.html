  Goalie // Keeper

A goalie on a soccer team is one of the most difficult positions to play. In this position, the goalie is making all the saves and has a number one priority of not letting one ball in the net. A Goalie, almost rarly can make a mistake, that doesn't end as a goal for the other team. So it is crucial for the goalie to not make a lot of mistakes. Additionally, the goalie has the best view on the field, so he/she should be communitcating to their defenders.

Position Name // Notation Role Notable Player(s)
Goalkeeper // GK Saves the shots. Positioned near the goal or on the ball to either save a shot or to take the ball from a players feet. Manuel Neuer, David de Gea, Keylor Navas
