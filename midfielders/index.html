  Midfielders

The midfielders are often the passmasters of a team. As the connect the defense to the forwards, they will often have the most assists and passes among the team. Their job is to send through balls to the forwards to enable them to score goals. Although their strength is often in passing, many midfielders such as Kevin de Bruyne are able to find the back of the net often. Midfielders come with many roles, some of them more suited towards attacking (CAM, or Center Attacking Midfielder), some will stay defensive (CDM, Center Defensive Midfielder), and some will stay on the wings (LM & RM, Left & Right Midfielder).

Position Name // Notation Role Notable Player(s)
Center Attacking Mid // CAM The CAM looks to come up on the attack in order to assist the forwards and even score a goal sometimes. Kevin de Bruyne, Mesut Ozil, James Rodriguez
Left Mid & Right Mid // LM & RM The left mid and right mid, especially when there is no wingers up top, will look to take over the sideline and advance the attack down the line. David Beckham
Center Mid // CDM The CDM acts as the opposite of the CAM. while they will usually come up on the attack, the CDM is generally more defensive, and will stay further back than the CAM. Paul Pogba, Casemiro
