  Defenders

On every team, defenders are the last line of defense except for the goalie. Defenders have to be able to be quick, calm, and able to take the ball away from, or "tackle" as it is officially called, an attacker. A successful tackle will see the attacker disposessed, but will not foul the attacker. Many defenders will also come up on the attack to aid the attacking players in scoring goals.

Position Name // Notation Role Notable Player(s)
Center Back // CB As the heart of the defense, the center back is responible for directing his back line to ensure the goalie doesn't need to make a save. Sergio Ramos, Gerard Pique, Diego Godin
Left & Right Back // LB & RB Playing next to the center backs, the left and right backs look to secure the wide areas of the field. It is not rare to see a left or right back go up the wings during an attack. Marcelo, Dani Alves, Alex Sandro
