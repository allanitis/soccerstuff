3D Printing With Printrbot and Cura

Our 3D printers took a while to get working, but now that they are working printing out 3D models is super easy. In the near future, students will be able to request their own prints for a small price.

Models can be found on Thingiverse or designed in a CAD suite such as Fusion 360 or Tinkercad.
